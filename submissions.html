1st Issue // Late Capitalism: Symptoms, Management, & Interventions

The first issue is published! However, if you want to contribute something this round, please do, and I will add it to the issue in the next couple of weeks. The second issue theme and submission call is upcoming.

We are interested in collecting images, video, audio, writings, and ideas for creating an zine issue that addresses issues in late capitalism. You may submit any material that addresses your anxieties about the present/future, the violent history of the development of the modern world, and injustices you would like to address. What kinds of coping mechanisms do you use? Submissions can also take a constructive approach to bringing up theories and methods for addressing inequalities and strategies for structural change. Your submissions may also capture the "essence" of this late capitalist stage in its vernacular, imagery and language. Perhaps there are aspects you would like to celebrate. Send them in!
